ABOUT

How big is the Solar System? Is the Earth a unique planet? Have we found evidence of any other life?

SpacePro is an interactive environment designed to entertain and educate anyone interested in learning more about our Solar System. The Home Page features a selection of facts about the Universe scattered amongst a representation of the Solar System where users may select a planet of their choosing in order to navigate to the information page of this planet. Each planet's information page includes the planet's profile and 10 key facts about each planet. In the future, I plan to update these pages with short quizzes!

I made this website single-handedly as practise for web design and development, and would appreciate if you could contact me with any further suggestions or necessary fixes! :)
